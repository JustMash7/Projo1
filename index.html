SPORTS

Sports refer to physical activities or games that involve skill, physical exertion, and often competition. They are usually governed by a set of rules or customs, and they can be played individually or as part of a team

Essence of Sports

In essence, sports represent a broad range of physical activities that are enjoyed for recreation, health, competition, and entertainment, playing a key role in personal development, social interaction, and cultural expression.

Types of Sports

Below are some of the sports known worldwide:

  • Soccer/Football
  • Basketball
  • Mixed Martial Arts

Soccer/Football

Soccer, also known as football outside of North America, is a team sport played by two teams of eleven players each, with the objective of scoring goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's goal. It is the world's most popular sport, played and watched by millions of people across the globe.

Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played by two teams, typically of five players each, with the goal of scoring points by shooting a ball through the opposing team's hoop (basket). It is one of the most popular sports worldwide, known for its high energy, athleticism, and entertainment value.

MMA

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) is a full-contact combat sport that allows a wide variety of fighting techniques and skills from different martial arts disciplines. It is one of the most versatile and dynamic sports, combining elements from disciplines such as boxing, Brazilian jiu-jitsu (BJJ), wrestling, Muay Thai, kickboxing, karate, judo, and more.

Importance of Sports

Why Sports is important:

  1. Physical Activity and Exercise
  2. Mental and Emotional Development
  3. Entertainment and Enjoyment
